An exclusive collection of five beautifully designed new-build homes located in the charming town of Edenbridge, Kent. Thoughtfully developed to blend modern living with traditional architectural character, these stylish two-bedroom properties offer high-quality finishes and well-planned layouts ideal for first-time buyers, downsizers, or investors.


Each home has been carefully designed to maximise space and natural light, featuring contemporary interiors, modern kitchens, and well-proportioned living areas perfect for everyday living and entertaining. The properties provide two comfortable bedrooms along with modern bathrooms finished to a high specification.


Externally, the homes feature attractive brick elevations with traditional detailing, landscaped surroundings, and allocated parking. Situated in a convenient location within Edenbridge, residents will benefit from excellent local amenities, transport links, and the beautiful Kent countryside nearby.


This small and thoughtfully designed development offers a rare opportunity to own a brand-new home in a desirable and well-connected location.
