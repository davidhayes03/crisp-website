Cherry Bryn.

A landmark residential project

Property Type Detached
Bedrooms z
Bathrooms. 4
Status In Progress

A stunning newly built contemporary home, thoughtfully designed and constructed following the demolition of the previous property. This impressive residence offers spacious and modern living across multiple levels.


The property features four generously sized bedrooms and five beautifully appointed bathrooms, providing exceptional comfort and privacy for family living and guests. The heart of the home is a spectacular open-plan kitchen and living area, finished to a high specification and designed for both everyday living and entertaining.


In addition, a separate family room offers a versatile space ideal for relaxation or a secondary lounge.


The lower ground floor boasts a superb basement level including a dedicated cinema room and a substantial gym area, creating the perfect environment for entertainment and wellbeing.


Finished in a sleek modern style throughout, this exceptional home combines contemporary design with practical family living.


Current photos showcase the build in progress, highlighting the transformation and scale of the project. More final images will be added as the property nears completion, so please check back soon to see the finished results.
